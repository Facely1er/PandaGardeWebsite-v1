🛡️ Password Safety

🔐
Keep Your Passwords Secret!
Passwords are like special keys to your digital home. Never share them with friends, and make them strong and unique!
Remember: A strong password has letters, numbers, and symbols!

🤐 Personal Information

👤
Keep Personal Info Private!
Never share your full name, address, phone number, or school name with strangers online. This information belongs only to you!
Safe to share: Your favorite color, favorite food, or favorite game

🚫 Stranger Danger

👥
Never Talk to Strangers Online!
If someone you don't know tries to talk to you online, tell a trusted adult immediately. Never meet up with someone you met online!
Remember: Real friends are people you know in real life!

📱 App Safety

📲
Ask Before You Download!
Always ask a parent or teacher before downloading any apps or games. Some apps might not be safe for kids!
Safe apps: Ask your parents to help you find age-appropriate apps!

🆘 When to Ask for Help

🙋‍♀️
Tell a Trusted Adult!
If something online makes you feel uncomfortable, scared, or confused, tell a parent, teacher, or trusted adult right away!
You're not in trouble: It's always okay to ask for help!
