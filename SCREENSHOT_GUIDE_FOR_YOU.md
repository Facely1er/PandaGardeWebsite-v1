# 📸 Screenshot Guide - Follow These Steps

**Android Studio is open - here's exactly what to do:**

---

## 🎯 Quick Steps (5 Minutes)

### 1. In Android Studio - Start Emulator

**Left sidebar** → **Device Manager** tab:
- If device exists: Click **▶ Play** button
- If no device: Click **Create Device** → Choose **Pixel 5** → Start

**Wait** for emulator to boot (1-2 minutes)

---

### 2. Run Your App

**Top toolbar** → **Green Play button (▶)**:
- Make sure "app" is selected
- Click **Play**
- Wait for app to build and launch

---

### 3. Capture Screenshots

**For each screen in your app:**

1. **Navigate** to the screen (Family Dashboard, Progress, etc.)
2. **Look at emulator window** - find **📷 camera icon** in toolbar
3. **Click camera icon** → Screenshot captured
4. **Save dialog** appears:
   - Navigate to: `C:\Users\facel\Downloads\GitHub\PandaGardeWebsite-v1\play-store-assets\screenshots\`
   - Name: `play-store-screenshot-1.png` (then 2, 3, etc.)
   - Click **Save**

**Repeat** for at least 2 screens (recommended 4-5)

---
