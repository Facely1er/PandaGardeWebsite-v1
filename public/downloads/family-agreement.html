🛡️ Family Internet Agreement

A guide to safe and responsible internet use for our family

Date: _______________

📱 Device Rules

Screen Time: I will use devices for no more than _____ hours per day on weekdays and _____ hours on weekends.
Device Location: I will use devices in common areas of the house where adults can see what I'm doing.
Bedtime: I will turn off all devices at _____ PM and put them in the designated charging area.
Charging Station: All devices will be charged in the family charging station, not in bedrooms.

🔐 Privacy & Safety Rules

Personal Information: I will never share my full name, address, phone number, school name, or any other personal information online.
Passwords: I will create strong passwords and never share them with friends. I will let my parents know my passwords.
Photos & Videos: I will not post photos or videos of myself or others without permission from my parents.
Strangers: I will never talk to strangers online or meet up with anyone I met on the internet.
Reporting: If someone online makes me feel uncomfortable or asks for personal information, I will tell my parents immediately.

📚 Educational Use

Learning First: I will use the internet primarily for educational purposes and learning activities.
App Downloads: I will ask permission before downloading any new apps or games.
Website Access: I will only visit websites that are appropriate for my age and that my parents have approved.
Online Learning: I will participate in online learning activities and complete assignments on time.

🤝 Respectful Behavior

Kindness: I will be kind and respectful to others online, just like I would be in person.
No Bullying: I will not bully, tease, or be mean to others online.
Standing Up: If I see someone being bullied online, I will tell a trusted adult.
Think Before Posting: I will think carefully before posting anything online and ask myself if it's kind and appropriate.

⚖️ Consequences

Breaking Rules: If I break any of these rules, I understand that my internet privileges may be limited or taken away.
Learning Opportunity: If I make a mistake, I will use it as a learning opportunity to do better next time.
Open Communication: I will always be honest with my parents about my online activities.
